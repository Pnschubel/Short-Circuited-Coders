Ways to Stay Safe:

  • Wash hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

  • Do not touch your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.

  • Socially distance: staying at least 6ft away from other people when possible.

  • Wear a cloth face mask over your nose and mouth in public settings.

  • Do NOT wear a face mask if you're under the age of two.

  • Reserve medical masks for health care worker, sick people, and at-risk individuals.

  • Stay home whenever possible.

Fact vs. Fiction:

  • Fiction: You have the constitutional right not to wear a mask in public.

  • Fact: People are allowed to refuse service to people without face masks.

  • Fiction: Ingesting disinfectants can protect you from Covid-19.

  • Fact: Ingesting disinfectants is extremely dangerous and should not be done under any circumstances.

  • Fiction: Covid-19 will disappear in warmer weather.

  • Fact: Covid-19 continues to affect people during the summer months and in warmer climates.

  • Fiction: Pets can spread the virus

  • Fact: Currently, there is no evidence that household pets carry COVID-19, though you should still wash your hands after petting pets.
